# PR-18 — Dual-Horizon Score and Confidence

## Summary

PR-18 adds `DUAL_HORIZON_CONFIDENCE_SHADOW_V1`: a read-only, Shadow-only
analysis that keeps the persisted 13-week Champion score as strategic input,
calculates a four-week tactical score, and reports five auditable confidence
components. It does not change the Champion, persist a Shadow result, or
promote a challenger.

## Frozen Behavior

- Champion identity is unchanged: `champion-v1.0.0`, digest
  `17ad1ca8854b0fbd8e56d6255b7ee2f4fe8a85ae1a95a328ade46ffdff02a0cf`,
  `SHADOW_ONLY`, and `championChanged: false`. The strategic score is copied
  from the selected persisted formal snapshot and the base exposure comes from
  the unchanged portfolio mapper.
- The eight literal positive Champion weights remain `netliqTrend` 0.35,
  `impulse` 0.05, `credit` 0.06, `funding` 0.04, `rates` 0.05, `dollar` 0.18,
  `reserveAdequacy` 0.12, and `curve` 0.15. They are not renormalized.
- Strategic analysis remains 13 weeks. Tactical analysis replaces only
  `netliqTrend` using four weeks. Tactical score boundaries are inclusive:
  score at least 60 gives +0.10, score at most 40 gives -0.10, otherwise zero.
- Confidence is the equal 20% mean of completeness, freshness, governed
  current-regime sample size, major-factor agreement, and Raw/Smooth direction
  agreement. All component evidence is returned; missing required evidence is
  `DATA_INCOMPLETE`, never neutral confidence.
- Every database input shares one resolved cutoff and is strictly visible before
  it. Raw/Smooth is rebuilt from cadence- and freshness-valid governed PIT
  inputs; its frozen 66 to 170 weekly-level bounds and database sentinels fail
  closed.
- A positive tactical adjustment is blocked only below unrounded confidence
  60. The Shadow exposure is capped at 0.75 only below unrounded confidence
  40. Exactly 60 permits the positive adjustment and exactly 40 does not invoke
  the cap.

## Known Limitations

- The four- and 13-week horizons, 40/60 tactical boundaries, equal confidence
  weights, regime cap, and confidence guards are preregistered heuristics, not
  fitted performance results.
- Regime sample confidence measures governed evidence quantity, not forecast
  skill.
- Runtime PIT Raw/Smooth is a PR-11 evidence class distinct from the PR-11
  current-vintage research artifact. It neither rehabilitates that dropped
  challenger nor supplies promotion evidence.
- There is no unseen out-of-sample evidence and no promotion gate. Promotion
  would require a separately frozen protocol and matured forward outcomes.
- No production channel-mixing issue was observed. Formal weekly and
  provisional daily channels remain deliberately separate; this PR does not
  refactor either channel.
- The documented AST governance-test future-surface limitation remains a
  non-blocking follow-up hardening item.
- Bounded loaders intentionally return typed incomplete results when a work
  sentinel is exceeded; increasing a bound needs separate governed review.
